What the 2026 Syllabus Demands

The new HSK syllabus (《新版HSK考试大纲》, effective July 2026) raises the bar at Level 4. Unlike HSK 3 which focuses on basic daily needs, HSK 4 requires handling "有一定复杂度" (a certain level of complexity) across 25 communicative tasks, grouped here into four themes:

25 Communicative Tasks

👤 Personal & Social

  • 谈论某个人物 — Discuss a person
  • 日常言语交往 — Daily verbal interactions
  • 谈论情感话题 — Discuss emotions
  • 交流业余爱好 — Hobbies & leisure
  • 交流家庭生活 — Family life
  • 交流居住、社区情况 — Housing & community

🏃 Daily Life

  • 交流、处理日常事务 — Handle daily affairs
  • 介绍饮食情况 — Food & dining
  • 谈论交通出行 — Transportation
  • 交流购物体验 — Shopping experiences
  • 谈论就医、健康生活 — Health & medical
  • 谈论体育比赛 — Sports

🎓 Education & Work

  • 谈论教学、学习 — Education & learning
  • 交流校园生活 — Campus life
  • 谈论教育现象 — Education phenomena
  • 谈论工作情况 — Work situations
  • 介绍职业经历 — Career experiences

🌏 Society & World

  • 谈论自然情况 — Nature & geography
  • 谈论环保情况 — Environmental protection
  • 介绍新技术应用 — Technology
  • 介绍中国省市、民族 — Chinese provinces & ethnicities
  • 谈论经济现象 — Economic phenomena
  • 谈论社会现象 — Social phenomena
  • 介绍文艺形式 — Arts & entertainment
  • 讲述中外友好故事 — China-world friendship

New Grammar Patterns at Level 4

The official grammar syllabus adds significant complexity at Level 4. The patterns below are the highest-leverage ones to master before sitting the test:

  • 把字句2 — four new structures (tentative, completed, quantified, modified)
  • 被动句2 — using 叫/让 instead of just 被
  • 兼语句2 — causative and evaluative sentences
  • 比较句3 — "A不如B" and "跟…相比"
  • 双重否定句 — for emphasis
  • 复句 — concessive (尽管…但是), conditional (不管…都, 无论…都), hypothetical (要是…否则)

Section-by-Section Tips

Strategy advice from the question types most often missed. Combine these with the strategy guides in the toolkit above.

听力 Listening

Listen for meaning, not just words

The HSK 4 listening section plays each clip only once. The 判断对错 section tests inference — what the speaker really means, not what they literally said. Train yourself to ask "what does this imply?" rather than "what did I hear?"

Listening strategies →
阅读 Reading

Learn collocations, not just words

Fill-in-the-blank rewards collocations. Knowing 影响 means "influence" isn't enough — you need 对…产生影响. Sentence ordering follows structural templates: time/place → subject → action → result/comment.

8-Week Study Plan

Most learners pass HSK 4 in 8 weeks of focused work, given a solid HSK 3 foundation. Here's the proven sequence — and what to do in each phase.

Weeks 1–4 Build & Diagnose

Take one full mock per week under timed conditions. Spend twice as long reviewing wrong answers as you spent on the test — that's where learning happens. Build vocabulary on the side using flashcards.

Weeks 5–8 Target weak section

Focus on your weakest section. Listening weak? Replay audio and shadow dialogues. Reading weak? Drill grammar patterns. Writing weak? Memorize sentence templates and ordering patterns.

Final 2 weeks Build exam stamina

Take 2–3 full tests back-to-back to simulate exam conditions. Aim for 70%+ consistently — that gives you a 10-point cushion above the 60% pass line on test day.
