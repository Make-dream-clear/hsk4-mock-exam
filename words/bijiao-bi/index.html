Comparison / 比较

比较 vs 比

relatively/quite vs comparing A to B

Comparison / 对比

比较 bǐjiào比 bǐ
FunctionAdverb: 'relatively, quite'Preposition: A 比 B + adj.
Structure比较 + adj./v.A 比 B + adj. (+ 多了/一点儿)
ComparisonNo explicit comparison objectMust have two things being compared
DegreeCannot add 很/非常 before adj.Cannot add 很 after 比

Examples / 例句

比较: 今天比较冷，多穿点衣服。
Jīntiān bǐjiào lěng, duō chuān diǎn yīfu.
It's relatively cold today — wear more. (no comparison target)
比: 今天比昨天冷多了。
Jīntiān bǐ zuótiān lěng duō le.
Today is much colder than yesterday. (comparing two days)
Quick rule: 比较 = 'relatively' (standalone, no B needed). 比 = 'than' (must have A 比 B). Common error: ×今天比昨天比较冷 — don't use both together.

Quick Quiz / 小测验

这个城市的交通方便。
No comparison target — just 'relatively convenient' → 比较.
坐飞机坐火车快。
Comparing plane vs train → A 比 B structure.
他的中文水平高，能看中文小说。
No second item to compare — 'quite high' → 比较.
