Adverbs

更 vs 还

even more (degree) vs also/still (addition)

Comparison / 对比

更 gèng还 hái
更Even more: 更好, 比…更…Intensifies comparison
还Still: 还在; also: 还有Surprisingly: 比他还高
Key diff更 = higher degree还 = addition or surprise
With 比比A更B = even more B than A比A还B = even more B (surprising)

Examples / 例句

更: 今天比昨天更冷了。
Jīntiān bǐ zuótiān gèng lěng le.
Today is even colder than yesterday.
还: 他会说中文，还会说日语。/ 我也想去。
Tā huì shuō zhōngwén, hái huì shuō rìyǔ. / Wǒ yě xiǎng qù.
He speaks Chinese and also Japanese. / I also want to go.
Quick rule: 更 = EVEN MORE (intensifies). 还 = ALSO/STILL (adds). With 比: 更 emphasizes degree, 还 implies surprise.

Quick Quiz / 小测验

这次考试难，很多人没通过。
Even more difficult → 更.
除了中文，他学了日语。
Also learned → 还.
这件事我一点儿不知道。
Emphasis (not at all) → 一点儿也不.
