Modal Verbs

应该 vs 必须

should vs must — advice vs obligation

Comparison / 对比

应该 yīnggāi必须 bìxū
StrengthShould (advice)Must (no choice)
Negative不应该 = shouldn't不必/不用 = don't need to
Probability他应该到了 = probably arrivedNo probability use
ToneAdvisoryMandatory

Examples / 例句

应该: 你应该多运动，对身体好。
Nǐ yīnggāi duō yùndòng, duì shēntǐ hǎo.
You should exercise more.
必须: 考试时必须关掉手机。
Kǎoshì shí bìxū guāndiào shǒujī.
You must turn off your phone during exams.
Quick rule: 应该 = should (advice, can decline). 必须 = must (rule). Also: 应该 means probably (他应该到家了), 必须 never does.
