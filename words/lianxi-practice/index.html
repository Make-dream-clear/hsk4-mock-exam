Verbs / 动词

联系 vs 练习

contact/connect vs practice — tones and meanings differ

Comparison / 对比

联系 liánxì练习 liànxí
MeaningTo contact; connection/relationshipTo practice; exercise/drill
Toneslián xì (2nd + 4th)liàn xí (4th + 2nd)
Object联系 + person: 联系他练习 + skill: 练习口语
As noun保持联系 (stay in contact)做练习 (do exercises)

Examples / 例句

联系: 如果有问题，请随时联系我。
Rúguǒ yǒu wèntí, qǐng suíshí liánxì wǒ.
If you have questions, contact me anytime.
练习: 每天练习半个小时口语，进步会很快。
Měitiān liànxí bàn ge xiǎoshí kǒuyǔ, jìnbù huì hěn kuài.
Practice speaking for half an hour daily and you'll improve fast.
Quick rule: 联系 (2nd-4th tone) = contact a PERSON. 练习 (4th-2nd tone) = practice a SKILL. The tones are swapped — listen carefully in the listening section.

Quick Quiz / 小测验

请把你的电话号码给我，方便以后。
Exchanging numbers to stay in contact → 联系.
为了准备HSK考试，我每天听力。
Practicing listening skills → 练习.
我们已经很久没有了，不知道她最近怎么样。
Haven't been in contact → 联系.
