Conjunctions / 连词

而且 vs 并且

moreover/and — progressive vs parallel

Comparison / 对比

而且 érqiě并且 bìngqiě
LogicProgressive: and furthermore (A → more B)Parallel: and also (A + B side by side)
EmphasisThe second clause is MORE importantBoth clauses are equally weighted
Common pair不但…而且……并且… (standalone)
RegisterNeutralSlightly more formal

Examples / 例句

而且: 这里的菜不但好吃，而且很便宜。
Zhèlǐ de cài búdàn hǎochī, érqiě hěn piányi.
The food here is not only delicious, but also cheap. (progressive: cheap is bonus info)
并且: 请仔细阅读合同，并且签上你的名字。
Qǐng zǐxì yuèdú hétong, bìngqiě qiān shàng nǐ de míngzì.
Please read the contract carefully and sign your name. (parallel: two equal actions)
Quick rule: 而且 = and MOREOVER (the second part adds something impressive or important). 并且 = and ALSO (two actions of equal weight, often instructions). If paired with 不但, always use 而且.

Quick Quiz / 小测验

请填写这张表格，交给前台。
Two equal actions (fill in + hand in) → 并且.
他不但通过了考试，得了第一名。
Progressive (passed → and even got 1st place!) → 而且.
这个方案既节约成本，提高了效率。
Two parallel benefits → 并且. (而且 also acceptable with progressive reading.)
