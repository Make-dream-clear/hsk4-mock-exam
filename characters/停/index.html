HSK 4 Words Containing 停

停 tíng to stop; to cease
前面有红灯，请停车。
Qiánmiàn yǒu hóngdēng, qǐng tíng chē.
There's a red light ahead, please stop.
停车 tíngchē to park a car; to stop
这里不能停车。
Zhèlǐ bù néng tíngchē.
You can't park here.
停车场 tíngchēchǎng parking lot
停车场已经满了。
Tíngchēchǎng yǐjīng mǎn le.
The parking lot is already full.
停止 tíngzhǐ to stop; to cease
雨已经停止了。
Yǔ yǐjīng tíngzhǐ le.
The rain has already stopped.
暂停 zàntíng to pause; temporar y suspension
比赛暂停了五分钟。
Bǐsài zàntíng le wǔ fēnzhōng.
The game was paused for five minutes.

About the character 停

停 (tíng) means to stop; to halt; to park. It is one of the 150 characters HSK 4 expects you to write from memory. Practice the stroke order until it feels automatic — most learners need 5–10 successful traces before a character "sticks".
