Official Syllabus Task

介绍文艺形式、活动、作品 — Arts & Entertainment

Discuss novels, movies, theater, performances, competitions, and introduce artists and their works.

19
Words
4
Skills Tested
Listening Speaking Reading Writing

Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于某种文艺形式、文艺活动、文艺作品创作者及其作品等有一定复杂度的询问。如某部小说、电影、话剧的大致内容、某场文艺表演、某位歌手、作家等。
