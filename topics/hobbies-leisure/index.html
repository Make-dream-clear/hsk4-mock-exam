交流业余爱好、休闲度假 — Hobbies & Leisure

Discuss leisure activities, reading, internet activities, sports, fitness, travel, and parties. Share feelings and opinions about these activities.

能听懂关于休闲活动情况及感受、看法的有一定复杂度的询问。如阅读、网络活动、运动、健身、旅行、聚会等。
