About the HSK 4 Vocabulary List

The HSK 4 vocabulary list contains approximately 1,000 words that build upon the 600 words from HSK 1-3. Mastering these words is essential for passing the HSK Level 4 exam, which certifies that you can discuss a wide range of topics in Chinese and communicate fluently with native speakers. The vocabulary covers everyday topics including work, education, travel, health, emotions, and social interactions.

This interactive vocabulary tool lets you study all HSK 4 words with pinyin pronunciation, English translations, and example sentences showing each word in context. Use the flashcard mode for active recall practice, take quizzes to test your knowledge, and track your progress as you master each word. All your progress is saved locally in your browser, so you can pick up where you left off anytime.

Created by Mandarin Zone, a Chinese language school in Beijing with over 5,000 students and a 98% HSK pass rate since 2008. For personalized HSK 4 preparation with experienced teachers, visit our website for online and in-person classes.
